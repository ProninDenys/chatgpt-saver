Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

Data Collection

ChatGPT Saver does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your saved ChatGPT responses are stored locally in your browser's storage.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API to save your data. This means your information never leaves your computer.

Permissions

The extension requires access to chat.openai.com to function properly. It does not access any other websites or your browsing history.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at pronindenys@yahoo.com
